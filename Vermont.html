West Dover, Vermont

West Dover is nearby Mount Snow! A great place to stay for snowboarders and skiers. The view from the town allows you to see the mountain on a snowy day.

Wilmington, Vermont

Wilmington is a town in Vermont known for their small shops. You can roam the streets for souvenirs such as maple syrup, candles, and snacks! The shops are all unique and adorable. They also have beautiful scenic paths you can walk along throughout the town. You may even see some individuals fishing in the nearby rivers.

Green Mountain, Vermont

Green Mountain is one of the hiking spot options. The hike includes water views as the streams go downwards. In the winter as the water freezes you can see icicles frozen in the mini waterfalls. Once you reach the top of the mountain you have a 360 view of the surrounding greenery.
